About DBS

Our Mission

At Doulos Biblical Seminary, our mission is to train, send out, and support dedicated missionaries to passionately preach the gospel and establish thriving churches among the unreached communities of the Indian sub-continent. We are committed to equipping our students with comprehensive biblical knowledge, practical ministry skills, and a heart for cross-cultural outreach. Through a transformative educational experience, we empower our graduates to become catalysts for spiritual transformation and agents of God's love, grace, and mercy.
