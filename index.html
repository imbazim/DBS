About DBS

Who Are We?

The Doulos Biblical Seminary, founded in the year 2000, is a center of Christian Studies rooted and grounded primarily in the Holy Bible. It is Pentecostal in its character and positioned to prepare students for Christian Ministry. This seminary is the tangible product of the vision of its founder, Rev. V.P. Jose. It is a wing of the Doulos International Ministries, Inc and a part of the well-known India Gospel Team.

While he was employed in Abu-Dhabi, United Arab Emirates in 1987, he was touched by the scripture Matthew 9:37-38 “the workers are few and the harvest is plentiful” and he recognized God’s call on him to work in the Lord’s vineyard. He decided to resign from his employment, left UAE and joined the Hebron Bible College, Kumbanad, Kerala as a part of his preparation for the ministry and on successful completion of a rigorous program of study he graduated from the College with a B. Th Degree. He has been a student of the word of God and studied at Eastern Baptist Theological Seminary Philadelphia for another B. Th and took his master’s degree from Newburgh Theological Seminary Indiana and is now pursuing his D. Min at the same seminary.
